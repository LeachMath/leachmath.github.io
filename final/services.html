Our Services

Food and Water

  • We feed and water your cat on each visit.
  • We optionally provide food.
  • We clean water dishes if necessary.

Litter

  • We scoop litter boxes on each visit.
  • We change litter once per week.
  • You must provide the litter.

Photo Updates

We know you like to see how your cat is doing while you're away, so we can send you a photo of your cat(s) when we visit. Photos of two cats are included. Photos of additional cats are $0.50 per photos.

Other Services

We can provide other small services during our visit. Common services include

  • Watering plants
  • Checking mail
